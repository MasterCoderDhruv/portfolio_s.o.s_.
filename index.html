Story Of Success

I am always inclined towards online games and mobile games. However, my father was not very happy with this as he always felts that physical games are always better than online. So he often takes me out to the sports field. However, with the pandemic, we were locked in the home. The School was also shut down due to government restrictions. With no options, I started playing mobile games regularly. While playing games, I think, what if I can have few more options of controlling my car in racing games. I use to ask my father if I can make changes in-game by inserting new opportunities, and his answer uses to be to grow up and learn to code. The response made me more curious to search about coding on the internet, and I came across White Hat Jr. I researched to understand reviews and feedback and went back to my father seeking his consent to enroll. He tested me for the next few days to ascertain if I was interested. He later realized that the risk of gaming addiction could convert into opportunity by enrolling me in White Hat Jr., and then my journey in the coding world started with White Hat Jr on 06th Aug 2020.
